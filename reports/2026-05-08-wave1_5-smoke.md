## Cross-stage contract verification

S2 reads from `signals_raw`. The integrated schema (H2) names the URL column
`url` and `canonical_url`, NOT `source_url`. S2's reader at
`scripts/discovery/stage2_verify_sources.py` introspects `PRAGMA table_info`
and accepts either `url` or `source_url`, so the contract works as-is on
the integrated branch (cross-conflict #1 resolved at code level).
